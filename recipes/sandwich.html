Special Ryan Sandwich Recipe

Description

Ryan's speciality sandwiches are so great!!!- Everyone

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Protein (Fish, Veggie Hot Dogs, Tofu, etc.)
  • Vegetables/Garnishes (E.g. Almost expired guacamole)
  • Sauces (E.g. Sriacha + Mayo, Ketchup, Mustard, etc.)

Steps

  1. Cook protein
  2. Toast bread
  3. Put protein on bread
  4. Add vegetables/garnishes
  5. Add sauces such as ketchup + mustard
  6. Enjoy with your taste buds!
