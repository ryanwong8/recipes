Special Ryan Tofu Rice Recipe

Description

Literally rice and tofu

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Vegetables (E.g. Raw Spinach because I'm too lazy to cook them)
  • Rice
  • Sauces (E.g. Hoisin + Sriracha)

Steps

  1. Cook or air-fry tofu
  2. Cook rice
  3. Put tofu on rice
  4. Put veggies on
  5. Add sauces
  6. Enjoy with your taste buds!
