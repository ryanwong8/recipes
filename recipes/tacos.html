Special Ryan Tacos Recipe

Description

Ryan's speciality tacos are sublime and unique!!! Easy to make and excellent tasting! Yummy & delectable!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Protein (Fish, Veggie Hot Dogs, Tofu, etc.)
  • Vegetables/Garnishes (E.g. Raw Onions)
  • Rice
  • Sauces (E.g. Sriacha + Mayo, Ketchup, Mustard, etc.)

Steps

  1. Cook protein
  2. Cook rice
  3. Put tortilla on skillet
  4. Put protein and rice on skillet
  5. Add garnishes
  6. Add sauces such as sriracha + mayo
  7. Enjoy with your taste buds!

Alternative Version: Hot Dog Tacos
