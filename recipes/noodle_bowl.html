Special Ryan Noodle Bowl Recipe

Description

Noodle in Bowl haha

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodle Bag(s)
  • Protein (Impossible Meat, Fish, Veggie Hot Dogs, Tofu, etc.)
  • (Optional) Vegetables/Garnishes (E.g. Raw Onions)
  • (Optional) Sauces (E.g. Sriacha + Mayo, Ketchup, Mustard, etc.)

Steps

  1. Cook protein
  2. Boil instant noodles in pot
  3. Throw away sauce packets because Ryan has no taste buds and it's bad for you
  4. Put protein on noodles
  5. (Optional) Add vegetables/garnishes if you have time
  6. (Optional) Add sauces such as ketchup + mustard if you have time
  7. Enjoy with your taste buds!
